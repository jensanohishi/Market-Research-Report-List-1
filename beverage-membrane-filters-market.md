Polypropylene membranes are known for their lightweight nature, while polytetrafluoroethylene provides excellent chemical resistance. Ceramic and metal membranes are utilized for high-temperature applications, while molecular sieve composite and glass membranes are effective for selective separation processes in beverage production.</p></p>
