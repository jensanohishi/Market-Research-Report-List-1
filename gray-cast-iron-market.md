<p><p>The Gray Cast Iron market comprises three primary types: Ferrite, Ferrite-Pearlite, and Pearlite. Ferrite is characterized by its soft, ductile nature, making it suitable for applications requiring high machinability. Ferrite-Pearlite combines the strength of pearlite with the ductility of ferrite, balancing both properties for versatile applications. Pearlite, known for its strength and hardness, is ideal for heavy-duty uses. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing their demand and pricing in the market based on mechanical properties and applications.</p></p>
