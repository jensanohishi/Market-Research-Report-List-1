<p><strong>The Hearing Screening Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OAE Hearing Screening Equipment</li><li>ABR Hearing Screening Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hearing screening equipment market primarily includes Otoacoustic Emission (OAE) and Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR) equipment. OAE hearing screening devices measure the inner ear’s response to sound, providing quick assessments of hearing function, often used in newborn screenings. ABR hearing screening equipment, on the other hand, evaluates the brain’s response to auditory stimuli, offering a more detailed analysis of auditory pathways, crucial for diagnosing hearing issues in infants and other populations. Both types are essential for early detection and intervention.</p></p>
