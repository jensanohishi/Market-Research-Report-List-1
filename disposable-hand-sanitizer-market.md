Spray sanitizers provide a convenient, mist-like application, allowing for quick coverage of the hands. Gel sanitizers are thick, viscous formulations that adhere well to the skin, ensuring prolonged contact for effective germ elimination. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for convenience, portability, and ease of use in maintaining hand hygiene.</p></p>
