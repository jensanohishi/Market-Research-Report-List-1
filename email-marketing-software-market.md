<p><p>Email marketing software typically exists in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. On-premises email marketing software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing more control over data security and customization, but typically requires higher initial investment and IT resources for maintenance.</p></p>
