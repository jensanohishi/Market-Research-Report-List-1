<p><p>The data logging sound level meters market can be segmented into two main types based on signal output: AC signal output and DC signal output. AC signal output meters provide a continuous representation of sound levels as varying electrical signals corresponding to sound frequency and amplitude, ideal for dynamic monitoring. In contrast, DC signal output meters convert sound levels into a steady voltage for easier integration with digital systems, making them suitable for long-term data collection and analysis in noise management projects.</p></p>
