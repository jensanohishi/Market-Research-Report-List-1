<p><p>The Point of Care Pregnancy and Fertility Testing market includes various tests that assess reproductive health. The Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG) urine test is commonly used to confirm pregnancy by detecting hCG levels. The FSH urine test assesses hormone levels to evaluate ovarian function and fertility issues. The hCG blood test provides a more precise measurement of hCG for pregnancy confirmation or monitoring. Lastly, the Luteinizing Hormone (LH) urine test helps predict ovulation, aiding in fertility planning.</p></p>
