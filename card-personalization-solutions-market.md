<p><p>Card personalization solutions encompass various printing technologies, primarily including dye sublimation, retransfer, and inkjet. Dye sublimation uses heat to transfer dye onto cards, offering vibrant colors and high durability, making it ideal for producing large volumes of cards efficiently. Retransfer printing involves printing images onto a film and then transferring them to the card surface, providing superior image quality and edge-to-edge printing. Inkjet technology utilizes liquid ink for direct printing, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness for smaller runs and customized designs.</p></p>
