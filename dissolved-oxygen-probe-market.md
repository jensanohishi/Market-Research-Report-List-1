<p><p>The dissolved oxygen probe market includes several types, primarily galvanic and polarographic probes, along with other variants. Galvanic probes utilize an electrochemical reaction for measurement, offering continuous readings without the need for external power. Polarographic probes require an external power source to operate but provide rapid and accurate measurements. Other types may include optical and fluorescence-based sensors, catering to specific industry needs. Each type is utilized across applications like wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
