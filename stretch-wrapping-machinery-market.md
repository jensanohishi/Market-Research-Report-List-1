<p><p>Stretch wrapping machinery is categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic types based on operation and efficiency. Semi-automatic machines require manual labor for positioning the load and often involve operators to start and stop the wrapping process. In contrast, fully automatic machines handle the entire wrapping process autonomously, reducing labor costs and improving speed. These distinctions cater to varying business needs, with semi-automatics suited for lower-volume operations and fully automatics ideal for high-volume production environments, maximizing productivity and consistency.</p></p>
