<p><p>The water filtration market is primarily divided into two types: whole-house water filtration systems and point-of-use water filtration systems. Whole-house systems are installed at the main water supply to filter all water entering a home, ensuring safe drinking and usage throughout the premises. In contrast, point-of-use systems are smaller, designed to filter water directly at the tap or specific appliances, providing immediate access to clean water. Both types address different filtration needs and preferences for residential consumers.</p></p>
