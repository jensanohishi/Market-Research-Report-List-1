<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Embedded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic film mulching machine market is divided into two main types: compression type and embedded type. The compression type uses high pressure to apply plastic film efficiently over the soil, enhancing crop growth by retaining moisture and controlling weeds. In contrast, the embedded type incorporates the plastic film directly into the soil during planting, promoting better soil aeration and nutrient retention. Both types aim to improve agricultural productivity while reducing manual labor in the farming process.</p></p>
