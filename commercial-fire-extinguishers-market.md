<p><p>The commercial fire extinguishers market encompasses various types designed to combat specific fire hazards. Dry chemical extinguishers are versatile, effective against Class A, B, and C fires, using a powder to suppress flames. Foam extinguishers efficiently tackle flammable liquid fires by creating a barrier, while carbon dioxide extinguishers are ideal for electrical and flammable liquid fires, displacing oxygen to extinguish flames. Water extinguishers, suitable for solid combustible materials, use water to cool and extinguish fires, making them a common choice in commercial settings.</p></p>
