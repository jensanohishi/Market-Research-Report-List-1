<p><p>Sales enablement software comes in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted online, allowing for seamless access, real-time updates, and easier collaboration across teams, making them ideal for organizations with remote or dispersed workforces. Conversely, on-premise solutions are installed locally on company servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, they may require more maintenance and upfront investment. Businesses choose based on their specific needs, resources, and security concerns.</p></p>
