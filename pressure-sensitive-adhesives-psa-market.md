<p><p>Pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) are adhesive substances that form a bond when pressure is applied, without the need for heat or solvents. The market includes elastomeric PSAs, which offer flexibility and excellent adhesion to various surfaces, making them suitable for applications like tapes and labels. Resin type PSAs, on the other hand, provide strong bonding properties and are commonly used in industries requiring durability and resistance to temperature changes. Both types cater to diverse applications across packaging, automotive, medical, and construction sectors.</p></p>
