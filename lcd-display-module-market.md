<p><p>The LCD Display Module market encompasses various types, including TN (Twisted Nematic), STN (Super Twisted Nematic), VA (Vertical Alignment), and TFT (Thin Film Transistor) displays. TN panels offer fast response times at a lower cost but have limited viewing angles. STN screens provide better contrast and viewing angles, suitable for simple applications. VA displays excel in color reproduction and contrast but may have slower response times. TFT technology delivers high-quality images with vibrant colors and better performance, making it ideal for advanced applications. Other types include less common technologies used in niche markets.</p></p>
