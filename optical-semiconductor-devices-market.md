<p><p>The optical semiconductor devices market can be divided into two main types: luminescent devices and light-receiving devices. Luminescent devices, such as light-emitting diodes (LEDs) and laser diodes, convert electrical energy into light, serving applications in displays, lighting, and communication. Light-receiving devices, including photodiodes and phototransistors, detect and convert light signals into electrical energy, playing crucial roles in sensors, imaging, and optical communication systems. Together, these devices are essential in various industries, enhancing technology and enabling efficient light manipulation.</p></p>
