<p><p>The toric soft contact lenses market includes two main types: conventional hydrogel materials and highly breathable silicone hydrogel materials. Conventional hydrogel lenses offer a comfortable fit and are often more affordable, but they have lower oxygen permeability. In contrast, highly breathable silicone hydrogel lenses allow significantly more oxygen to reach the cornea, improving eye health and comfort, especially for extended wear. Both types cater to individuals with astigmatism, ensuring clear vision and enhanced wearability based on user preferences and needs.</p></p>
