<p><p>Day trading software comes in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based platforms operate via the internet, allowing traders to access their accounts and execute trades from anywhere, benefiting from automatic updates and scalability. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on a trader's computer, offering more control and customization but limiting accessibility. While cloud solutions typically provide ease of use and collaboration, on-premises options may appeal to those who prioritize security and performance management.</p></p>
