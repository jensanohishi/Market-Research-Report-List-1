<p><p>Concrete Weight Coatings (CWC) are used to provide ballast and protection for underwater pipelines and cables. The market encompasses various application methods: </p><p>1. **Jet Impact Method** applies high-velocity jets to secure coatings.</p><p>2. **Extrusion Winding Method** layers concrete through a continuous extrusion process.</p><p>3. **Sliding Mode Method** utilizes a sliding mechanism to apply coating evenly.</p><p>4. **Centrifugal Grouting Method** employs centrifugal force to distribute concrete uniformly around the pipeline, ensuring durability and stability in marine environments.</p></p>
