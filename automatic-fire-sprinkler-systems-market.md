<p><strong>The Automatic Fire Sprinkler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensitive Smoke</li><li>Sensitive Temperature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic fire sprinkler systems can be categorized into two main market types: sensitive smoke and sensitive temperature systems. Sensitive smoke systems utilize detectors that respond to smoke particles, enabling quick activation to suppress fires in their early stages. Conversely, sensitive temperature systems rely on heat detection; they activate when the surrounding temperature exceeds a predetermined threshold. Both types are crucial for enhancing fire safety in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, protecting lives and property effectively.</p></p>
