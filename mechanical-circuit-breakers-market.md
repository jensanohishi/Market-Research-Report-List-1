<p><p>The mechanical circuit breakers market is segmented into several types based on operating mediums. Air circuit breakers use air as an insulating medium and are common for low-voltage applications. Oil circuit breakers employ oil for insulation and arc extinguishing, suitable for medium-voltage systems. Sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) circuit breakers utilize SF6 gas, offering superior insulation and are effective in high-voltage environments. Vacuum circuit breakers operate in a vacuum, ensuring minimal wear. Other circuit breakers encompass diverse technologies for specific applications.</p></p>
