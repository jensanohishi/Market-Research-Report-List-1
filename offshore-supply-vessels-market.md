<p><p>The offshore supply vessels market includes various specialized vessel types. Anchor Handling Tug Supply Vessels (AHTS) are designed to assist in anchor handling and towing operations. Platform Supply Vessels (PSV) transport supplies to offshore platforms. Multipurpose Support Vessels (MPSV) offer versatile support for various offshore activities, including construction and maintenance. Standby & Rescue Vessels (SRV) provide emergency support and crew change services. Seismic Vessels are equipped for geophysical surveys, essential for oil and gas exploration, enhancing their role in offshore operations.</p></p>
