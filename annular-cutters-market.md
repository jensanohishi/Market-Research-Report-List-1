<p><strong>The Annular Cutters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HSS Type</li><li>TCT Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The annular cutters market is primarily divided into two main types: HSS (High-Speed Steel) and TCT (Tungsten Carbide Tipped). HSS cutters are known for their cost-effectiveness and versatility, suitable for drilling metal and softer materials. Conversely, TCT cutters offer enhanced durability and performance, excelling in tougher materials like stainless steel and harder metals. Both types cater to different industrial applications, with HSS being favored for general use, while TCT is preferred for heavy-duty tasks requiring precision and longevity.</p></p>
