<p><strong>The Electrical Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical isolators market comprises several types, including single break, double break, and pantograph isolators. Single break isolators feature a single point of contact, suitable for lower voltage applications. Double break isolators provide two points of disconnection for improved safety and reliability, making them ideal for higher voltage settings. Pantograph isolators utilize a unique design that allows them to be mounted on overhead lines, enabling easy operation and flexibility in space-constrained environments. Each type serves specific operational needs in electrical systems.</p></p>
