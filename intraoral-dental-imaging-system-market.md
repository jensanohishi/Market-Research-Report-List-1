<p><ul><li>Bite Wing X-Rays</li><li>Apical X-Rays</li><li>Occlusal X-Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraoral dental imaging system market encompasses various types of X-ray imaging techniques used for diagnosing dental issues. Bite wing X-rays capture the upper and lower teeth in a single image, primarily for detecting cavities between teeth. Apical X-rays focus on the root structure of teeth to identify issues below the gum line, such as infections. Occlusal X-rays provide a comprehensive view of the entire dental arch, helping to assess the relationship between teeth and the alignment of the jaw.</p></p>
