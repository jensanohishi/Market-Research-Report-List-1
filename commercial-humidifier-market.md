<p><strong>The Commercial Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial humidifier market includes two primary types: vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers produce steam by boiling water, effectively adding moisture to the air and enhancing indoor air quality. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse fine droplets of water into the air, increasing humidity levels without raising the temperature significantly. Both types are essential in various commercial environments, such as offices, warehouses, and manufacturing facilities, to maintain optimal humidity for health and productivity.</p></p>
