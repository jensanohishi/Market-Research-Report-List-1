<p><ul><li>Active RFID Tags</li><li>Passive RFID Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID label design software caters to two main market types: active RFID tags and passive RFID tags. Active RFID tags contain their own power source, enabling longer read ranges and the ability to transmit data over greater distances, making them suitable for asset tracking and real-time monitoring. In contrast, passive RFID tags rely on an external reader for power, offering a cost-effective solution for applications like inventory management and access control, though they have shorter read ranges compared to their active counterparts.</p></p>
