<p><strong>The Heart Lung Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump Heart Lung Machine</li><li>Double Roller Pump Heart Lung Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heart Lung Machine market is categorized into two main types: Single Roller Pump and Double Roller Pump machines. Single Roller Pump machines utilize a single roller to propel blood, generally offering a simpler design and ease of use, making them suitable for various surgical applications. In contrast, Double Roller Pump machines employ two rollers for enhanced blood flow control and reduced hemolysis, providing higher efficiency and precision during complex surgeries. Both types play vital roles in cardiac surgeries and critical care settings.</p></p>
