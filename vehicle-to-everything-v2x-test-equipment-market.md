<p><p>The Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) test equipment market comprises two primary types of connectivity: Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) and Cellular Connectivity. DSRC is a communication protocol designed for low-latency, high-reliability transmissions between vehicles and infrastructure, facilitating applications like collision avoidance and traffic management. Cellular Connectivity, such as 4G LTE or 5G, offers broader coverage and higher data rates, enabling advanced services like real-time traffic updates and vehicle-to-cloud interactions. Both play critical roles in enhancing vehicular communication and safety.</p></p>
