<p><p>RF SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) and BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters are crucial components in wireless communication systems. SAW filters utilize surface waves propagating along a piezoelectric substrate, offering compact size and moderate performance for applications like mobile phones and RFID. BAW filters, on the other hand, use bulk acoustic waves, providing better performance in terms of loss and frequency selectivity, making them ideal for higher frequency bands, such as 4G and 5G networks. Both filter types enhance signal integrity in complex communications.</p></p>
