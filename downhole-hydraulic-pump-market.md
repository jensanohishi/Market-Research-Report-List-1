<p><strong>The Downhole Hydraulic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating pumps (sucker-rod installation)</li><li>Jet pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The downhole hydraulic pump market primarily includes two types: reciprocating pumps and jet pumps. Reciprocating pumps, often referred to as sucker-rod installations, utilize a rod mechanism to lift fluids through a well, making them suitable for deeper applications with high viscosity fluids. In contrast, jet pumps employ high-speed jets of fluid to create a pressure differential, drawing fluid from the well. Each pump type offers distinct advantages, catering to specific operational needs within the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
